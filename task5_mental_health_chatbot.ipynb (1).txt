    "## 1. Reference: real fine-tuning pipeline (requires internet \u2014 not executed here)\n",
    "\n",
    "```python\n",
    "# pip install transformers datasets torch accelerate\n",
    "\n",
    "from datasets import load_dataset\n",
    "from transformers import (AutoTokenizer, AutoModelForCausalLM,\n",
    "                           TrainingArguments, Trainer, DataCollatorForLanguageModeling)\n",
    "\n",
    "dataset = load_dataset(\"empathetic_dialogues\")          # real EmpatheticDialogues data\n",
    "model_name = \"distilgpt2\"\n",
    "\n",
    "tokenizer = AutoTokenizer.from_pretrained(model_name)\n",
    "tokenizer.pad_token = tokenizer.eos_token\n",
    "model = AutoModelForCausalLM.from_pretrained(model_name)\n",
    "\n",
    "def format_example(ex):\n",
    "    # Prompt the model to respond gently and supportively to the \"context\" (user utterance)\n",
    "    text = f\"User: {ex['context']}\\nSupportive friend: {ex['utterance']}{tokenizer.eos_token}\"\n",
    "    return tokenizer(text, truncation=True, max_length=128)\n",
    "\n",
    "tokenized = dataset.map(format_example, remove_columns=dataset[\"train\"].column_names)\n",
    "collator = DataCollatorForLanguageModeling(tokenizer, mlm=False)\n",
    "\n",
    "args = TrainingArguments(\n",
    "    output_dir=\"./mh-chatbot-distilgpt2\",\n",
    "    per_device_train_batch_size=8,\n",
    "    num_train_epochs=3,\n",
    "    learning_rate=5e-5,\n",
    "    logging_steps=50,\n",
    "    save_strategy=\"epoch\",\n",
    ")\n",
    "\n",
    "trainer = Trainer(model=model, args=args, train_dataset=tokenized[\"train\"],\n",
    "                   data_collator=collator)\n",
    "trainer.train()\n",
    "trainer.save_model(\"./mh-chatbot-distilgpt2\")\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "This is the intended production approach \u2014 a small causal LM fine-tuned directly on empathetic\n",
    "human dialogues, so its generations naturally pick up a gentle, validating tone.\n"

    "### Empathetic response bank (gentle, validating tone \u2014 one template family per emotion)"
   ]
  },
  {
   "cell_type": "code",
   "metadata": {},
   "execution_count": 3,
   "source": [
    "RESPONSE_BANK = {\n",
    "    \"anxious\": [\n",
    "        \"That sounds really nerve-wracking. It makes sense to feel this way before something important \u2014 your feelings are valid, and you don't have to face it alone.\",\n",
    "        \"I hear how anxious this is making you. Try taking a slow breath with me \u2014 in for 4 counts, out for 6. What's one small thing that might help you feel a bit more prepared?\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "    \"stressed\": [\n",
    "        \"It sounds like you're carrying a lot right now. That's genuinely a lot to hold \u2014 it's okay to feel stretched thin. Is there one thing on your plate we could think through together?\",\n",
    "        \"That's a heavy load. You're allowed to take things one step at a time instead of all at once. What feels most urgent to you right now?\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "    \"lonely\": [\n",
    "        \"That sounds really hard, and I'm glad you told me. Feeling disconnected from others is painful, even when you're surrounded by people. I'm here with you right now.\",\n",
    "        \"Loneliness can feel so heavy. You deserve connection and care. Is there someone \u2014 even one person \u2014 you might feel comfortable reaching out to today?\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "    \"sad\": [\n",
    "        \"I'm really sorry you're going through this. It's okay to feel low \u2014 your feelings make sense given what you're carrying. I'm here to listen for as long as you need.\",\n",
    "        \"That sounds painful, and I want you to know it's okay to not be okay right now. Would it help to talk more about what's weighing on you?\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "    \"happy\": [\n",
    "        \"That's wonderful to hear \u2014 I'm genuinely happy for you! Savor this moment, you've earned it.\",\n",
    "        \"That's great news! It sounds like something you can be really proud of.\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "    \"angry\": [\n",
    "        \"That sounds really frustrating, and it's completely understandable to feel angry about that. Do you want to vent more, or think through what to do next?\",\n",
    "        \"I can hear how upsetting that is. Your frustration makes sense. Let's take a moment before deciding how to respond to it.\",\n",
    "    ],\n",
    "}\n",
    "\n",
    "# --- Safety guardrails ---\n",
    "CRISIS_KEYWORDS = [\"suicide\", \"kill myself\", \"self harm\", \"self-harm\", \"end my life\", \"want to die\"]\n",
    "MEDICAL_ADVICE_KEYWORDS = [\"medication\", \"dosage\", \"diagnose\", \"prescribe\", \"medicine\"]\n",
    "\n",
    "def safety_filter(user_text):\n",
    "    lowered = user_text.lower()\n",
    "    if any(k in lowered for k in CRISIS_KEYWORDS):\n",
    "        return (\"It sounds like you might be going through something really painful right now. \"\n",
    "                \"I'm not able to provide crisis support, but please reach out to a crisis line or \"\n",
    "                \"a trusted person right away \u2014 you deserve real, immediate support. In the US, you \"\n",
    "                \"can call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).\")\n",
    "    if any(k in lowered for k in MEDICAL_ADVICE_KEYWORDS):\n",
    "        return (\"I can't give medical advice like dosages or diagnoses \u2014 that's important to \"\n",
    "                \"discuss with a doctor or pharmacist. But I'm glad to listen and support you \"\n",
    "                \"emotionally with what you're going through.\")\n",
    "    return None\n",
    "\n",
    "def chatbot_reply(user_text):\n",
    "    guard = safety_filter(user_text)\n",
    "    if guard:\n",
    "        return guard\n",
    "    vec = vectorizer.transform([user_text])\n",
    "    emotion = clf.predict(vec)[0]\n",
    "    return random.choice(RESPONSE_BANK[emotion])\n",
    "\n",
    "print(\"Chatbot ready.\")\n"

    "## 4. Insights & Final Results\n",
    "\n",
    "- The classifier reliably separates six emotional categories (anxious, stressed, lonely, sad,\n",
    "  happy, angry) even from a small hand-labeled sample, because emotion-specific vocabulary is\n",
    "  fairly distinct (e.g. \"panicking\"/\"nervous\" vs \"alone\"/\"nobody understands\").\n",
    "- Safety guardrails intercept crisis language and requests for medical dosing/diagnosis **before**\n",
    "  they ever reach the empathy model, redirecting to appropriate real-world resources instead of\n",
    "  attempting to help directly \u2014 this mirrors the \"safety filters\" requirement from Task 4/5.\n",
    "- This offline classifier + template approach is a reasonable lightweight stand-in, but a genuine\n",
    "  fine-tuned generative model (Section 1) would produce more varied, context-specific replies\n",
    "  rather than templated ones \u2014 worth running on a machine with internet + GPU access for a\n",
